Terms & Conditions

Please read our rental terms carefully before booking your motorcycle or scooter.

  • Rental Duration

    Scooters and bikes are rented for the agreed period only. Extra hours beyond the rental period may incur additional charges. Please return the vehicle on time to avoid penalty fees.

  • Security Deposit

    A refundable security deposit is required before renting any vehicle. The deposit amount varies by bike model and rental duration. Full refund upon safe return of the vehicle without damages.

  • Fuel Policy

    All vehicles are provided with full fuel at the start of rental. Customers must return the vehicle with the same fuel level. If returned with less fuel, applicable fuel charges will be deducted from the security deposit.

  • Helmet & Safety Equipment

    Wearing helmets is mandatory for all riders and passengers as per Nepal traffic laws. We provide complimentary helmets with every rental. Riders are fully responsible for their own safety and must follow all traffic regulations.

  • Late Returns

    Late returns will be charged at an hourly rate based on the daily rental price. If the delay exceeds 6 hours, a full additional day's rental fee will apply. Please inform us in advance if you need to extend your rental period.

  • Damage & Accidents

    Any damages, accidents, or mechanical issues during the rental period are the renter's responsibility. Repair charges will be assessed and deducted from the security deposit. In case of major damage, additional costs may apply. Always ride safely and within your skill level.

  • Required Documents

    Valid identification is mandatory for all rentals. Acceptable documents include: Valid Motorcycle License, Passport (for international tourists), Nepali Citizenship ID (for locals), or Driver's License with photo ID. Photocopies will be taken for our records.

  • Legal Compliance

    Riders must follow all local traffic rules and regulations of Nepal. The rental agency is not liable for any traffic fines, violations, or legal issues incurred during the rental period. All fines and penalties are the sole responsibility of the renter.

  • Age Requirement

    Riders must be at least 18 years old with a valid motorcycle license. For certain high-capacity bikes, minimum age may be 21 years. Age verification is required at the time of booking.

  • Cancellation Policy

    Cancellations made 24 hours before the rental period are eligible for full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours of rental start time will incur a 50% cancellation fee. No-shows will forfeit the entire booking amount.
