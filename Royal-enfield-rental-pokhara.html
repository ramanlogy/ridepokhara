Popular Royal Enfield Routes from Pokhara

1. Upper Mustang Expedition (10-14 Days) - Himalayan 411 Recommended

The ultimate Royal Enfield adventure. Ride through the restricted "Forbidden Kingdom" to the ancient walled city of Lo Manthang. This route requires special permits (USD 500) and is only accessible May-October. The Himalayan 411's ground clearance and rugged build make it the ONLY rental bike we allow on this route.

2. Annapurna Circuit (7-10 Days) - Both Bikes Suitable

Circle the Annapurna massif via Manang and Thorong La Pass (5,416m). While the Classic 350 can handle most of this route, we strongly recommend the Himalayan 411 for the high-altitude sections and river crossings.

3. Pokhara to Kathmandu via Bandipur (2-3 Days) - Classic 350 Perfect

Scenic highway riding with stops at the hilltop town of Bandipur. The Classic 350's comfortable seating position and highway stability shine on this 200km paved route.

4. Ghorepani-Poon Hill Loop (3-4 Days) - Himalayan 411 Required

Rough mountain roads from Nayapul through rhododendron forests. The Himalayan's off-road capability is essential for this challenging but rewarding route.

Royal Enfield Rental FAQs

Can I take the Royal Enfield to India?

No, cross-border rentals are not permitted. The bikes must stay within Nepal.

What happens if the bike breaks down?

Call our 24/7 support line immediately. For mechanical failures due to normal wear (not rider negligence), we arrange repairs at no cost to you. We have mechanic partnerships in major towns along popular routes.

Do you offer Royal Enfield guided tours?

Yes! We can arrange fully guided Royal Enfield tours with support vehicles, permits, and accommodation included. Contact us for custom tour packages.

How much fuel does a Royal Enfield use?

Classic 350: Approximately 30-35 km/liter
Himalayan 411: Approximately 28-32 km/liter
(Fuel economy varies based on terrain and riding style)

Can beginners rent a Royal Enfield?

We require at least 6 months of motorcycle riding experience for Royal Enfields. These are powerful bikes (20-24 HP) and weigh 185-200kg. If you're new to motorcycles, we recommend starting with our 150cc bikes or scooters.
